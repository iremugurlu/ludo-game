How to play

1. Two, three, or four can play, without partnerships.

2. Each player rolls the die; the highest roller begins the game. Players alternate turns in a clockwise direction.

3. When a 6 is rolled, the player may choose to advance a token already in play, or may enter another staged token to its starting square. Rolling a 6 earns the player an additional or "bonus" roll in that turn.

4. The first to bring all their tokens to the finish wins the game.
